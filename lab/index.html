Synergistic Sensing and Computing System Lab

The Synergistic Sensing and Computing System (SSCS) Lab at North Carolina State University delves into advanced research topics situated at the crossroads of sensor hardware/architecture, signal processing, operating systems, algorithms, human factors, and their applicability in human-computer interaction, healthcare, and cybersecurity domains. Our research undertakings encompass a broad spectrum, ranging from the development of hardware/software foundations to the construction of pragmatic computer systems.
